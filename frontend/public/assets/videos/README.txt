Place your hero background video here as:

    hero-video.mp4

The Home page (Frontend/src/pages/website/Home/Home.jsx) references:
    src="/assets/videos/hero-video.mp4"

If the file is missing, the hero section background will appear black/empty.
This folder was missing from the project archive — that is why the video was
not appearing. Simply drop your hero-video.mp4 file in this folder and rebuild.
